About Me

I come from Egypt and I live in Hamburg, Germany since september 2016. it was hard at the beginning to start my web development career in Germany due to the language, but here I'm back again with a fresh start and ready for a new challenge in my life and my German now is so much better than before.

I've graduated from the Faculty of Computers and Information in Assiut University in Egypt at 2015. I got the Bachelor's Degree of Information Systems with Good grade. after that I started my career as Freelancer and I've done some projects to my friends. In 2016 I made a system to a construction company, I've developed this system with Laravel "A Great MVC PHP Framework" , this was for back-end. In front-end I've worked with HTML 5, CSS, Javascript, jQuery and Boootstrap 4. This System handles all the procedures of constructing Buildings by this company. it was full responsive from design.

At April 2018 I started a PHP Web Development Course AKA "Weiterbildung In German" in CBW.

At end of May 2018, I started building a Dentist Clinic Information System for a Dentist in Egypt, it handles everything from creating Patients, Diagnosis, Appointments and Payments. Skills Applied: Laravel, HTML 5, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, AJAX and Boootstrap 4.
